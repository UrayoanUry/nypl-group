Take a Hike Tours Does Things Differently

Nature lovers, join us on one of our several breathtaking Hawaiian Hiking and Adventure Tours taking you to some of our best island trails and lookout points. We offer Hiking, ATV, Ziplining and Helicopter Tours. Explore lush rainforests, waterfalls, volcanic craters, bird sanctuaries, ancient fishponds, deep valleys and much, much more. Surround yourself in Hawaiian culture while exploring the natural splendor, the ideal way to sightsee.


Our highly trained naturalist guides will provide you with entertaining commentary on your tour. Our guides are experts in the local geology, culture, plant and animal life as well as our island traditions. We also offer hotel pickup and delivery and refreshments to ensure that your Hawaiian Adventures will be a memorable and enjoyable experience. So ask questions and enjoy the one of a kind natural beauties that our spectacular islands have to offer.
